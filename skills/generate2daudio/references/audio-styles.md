# Audio Styles

Use this reference when choosing synthesis parameters.

- `retro`: square/triangle tones, short envelopes, light noise, mono.
- `chiptune`: arpeggios, stepped pitch changes, bright square waves.
- `fantasy`: softer attacks, bell-like sine layers, warmer fades.
- `sci_fi`: pitch sweeps, laser tones, filtered noise bursts.
- `cozy`: low volume, soft sine/triangle tones, no harsh noise.
- `arcade`: bright, quick, strong transient, simple pitch movement.
- `horror`: low drones, noisy impacts, slow fades; keep short SFX controlled.

Bundle defaults:

- `ui_pack`: select, confirm, cancel, error.
- `fireball_pack`: spell-cast, spell-loop, spell-hit.
- `platformer_pack`: jump, land, hit, pickup, powerup.
